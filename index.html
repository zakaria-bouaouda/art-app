I am BOUAOUDA Zakaria

I study Computer science engineering at Mahammadia School of engineers in Rabat. I am also an Artist, I can do many types of art, such as: Painting, Drawing and Photography.

I can say this talent is a gift from god, All the skills I have are learned by myself even if I didn't integrated any art academy. I am proud of this canvas even if they are not perfect. Nothing is perfect by the way!

I created this website to express my talent, my paintings and my art with the world.

I hope that you will like that; thanks for your visit !
